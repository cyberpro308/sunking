About Sunking

Who We Are

Sunking is more than just a solar brand – we are a movement to bring affordable, reliable, and sustainable energy to homes and businesses around the world. Since our founding, we’ve been dedicated to breaking energy barriers by providing innovative solar solutions that empower communities and drive progress.

With millions of products sold globally, Sunking has become a trusted leader in renewable energy. Our products range from home lighting systems to solar-powered appliances, all designed with one mission in mind: making clean energy accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

To brighten lives by delivering affordable, innovative, and reliable solar solutions that empower people, reduce energy poverty, and protect the planet.

Our Vision

A world where clean energy powers every home, school, and business – creating a brighter, more sustainable future for all.

Our Values

Innovation, Integrity, Sustainability, and Impact. These values guide everything we do, from design to customer experience, ensuring we deliver products that truly make a difference.
