Sun King is a movement to deliver affordable, reliable solar energy — empowering homes and businesses with clean power.

Who we are

Energy access that changes lives

Sun King is more than a solar brand — we are dedicated to breaking energy barriers by providing innovative solar solutions that empower communities and drive progress.

From home lighting systems to solar-powered appliances, our mission is simple: make clean, dependable energy accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our journey

Milestones that lit the way

A snapshot of how Sun King grew into one of the world's leading off-grid solar companies.

2007

Sun King is founded with a simple mission: replace kerosene lamps with clean, affordable solar light.

2012

Pay-as-you-go financing launches, making solar accessible to families on modest incomes.

2018

Expansion across Africa and Asia — millions of homes now powered by Sun King systems.

Today

Over 120 million lives brightened, with a growing range of solar appliances and inverter systems.
